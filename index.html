Builder Site

Welcome to the Component Builder information site.

There are two versions of the builder.

  • Builder Version 2 is the older builder for V2. It has the il-** namespace. This was started in 2019, and sunset on 2024. We recommend not using this unless your Content Management System is built for it.
  • Builder Version 3 is the newer builder for V3. It has the ilw-** namespace. This was started in 2024 and is currently being developed (as of 12/2025).
